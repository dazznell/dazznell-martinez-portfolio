— Sitemap Reorganization —

Top-level & Reasons

  • Camps | This was an addition/replacement for "2019 Workshops" and the "2019 Registration Information" buttons on the navigation because it encompasses both descriptions and all the information should be in the same page
  • Get Involved | This was an addition to the navigation menu because it important to showcase that this company is a non-profit organization. It is also a convention shown on other non-profit websites.
  • About Us | This is an addition/replacement for "What is it?", "Where is it?", "History", and "Contact" to lessen the visual noise. This was also a convention that's mostly used by many effective websites.

Sub-level & Reasons

  • Registration Info, Fees, Scholarships & Bursaries | These we're place under Camps because these are the main information that users will look for when inquiring about the camps offered by this organization.
  • Volunteer, Jobs | These are the only 2 ways that an external individual can get involved with the organization.
  • Contact, Faculty, Facilities, History, Newsletters | After interviewing a parent that will potentially register their child to one of the camps, these are the listed information they'd look for while searching for credibility and legitimacy. It also makes it easier for them to learn about organization outside of camps.
