— Purpose of Redesign —

We are given a chance to analyze an existing app to determine the ideal user flow and redesign the UI flow, via a clickable prototype. Instead of analyzing and recreating sitemaps, we will focus on the UI flow. We learned how to turn the discovery research into a proposed user flow, then convert this into a wireframe (UI flow) and then finally a prototype.
